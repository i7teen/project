This page is about showcasing some works of a Filipino street photographer

Anton Baello

, where he captures silly frames, patterns, light and shadows, emotions, depictions, and various forms of art framed through a lens.

I chose this topic since I fell in love with the beautiful and raw images street photography can offer; Everyday, I still wonder on the beauty each shot can make, the moment is so vivid, like a fiesta paused on the right time.
